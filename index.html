How to Install MyRemit APK

  1. Go to your device's settings.
  2. Navigate to Security or Applications (depending on your device).
  3. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" or "Install Unknown Apps".
  4. Locate the downloaded APK file in your file manager and tap to install.

If you encounter a warning from Google Play Protect, you may need to disable Play Protect temporarily while installing the app. Remember to re-enable it after installation for continued security.
